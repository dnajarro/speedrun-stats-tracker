Primary information:
- Total number of submitted runs
https://www.speedrun.com/api/v1/runs?max={max}&offset={offset}&game={game_id}
- Top 10 runs for Any% and/or 100%
https://www.speedrun.com/api/v1/leaderboards/{game_id}/category/{category_id}?top=10&embed=players
Then data['data']['players']['data'][0]['names']['international'] for the players' names
Then data['data']['runs'][0]['run']['status']['status'] == 'verified' to check verification
Then data['data']['runs'][0]['run']['times']['primary_t'] to get time in seconds
- Newest runs (limit 50?)
https://www.speedrun.com/api/v1/runs?game={game_id}&status=verified&orderby=verify-date&direction=desc for newest runs based on submission date


Secondary information:
- How much the top time has gone down since first recorded
https://www.speedrun.com/api/v1/leaderboards/{game_id}/category/{category_id}?top=1 and compare to the top time at first recording
- How many more runs have been submitted (accepted?) since first recorded
https://www.speedrun.com/api/v1/runs?game={game_id}&status=verified&orderby=verify-date&direction=desc and compare to number at first recording
